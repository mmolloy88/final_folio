History of care

I am a Sydney, NSW based retiree with previous work experience in aged care.

My journey started in 2013 when my son became ill with kidney failure. During this time I decided to turn away from a full-time work to focus on care.

2 Years later, I became a part-time carer with Home Instead and honed my skills, earning certification in first aid, manual handling, dementia care and achieving over 1000 hours of service.
